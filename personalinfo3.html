Last opinions

Il Violino, NYC, USA

Our first visit did not disappoint. We were party of 4 on a Sunday evening sitting at a window with a view of city life. Service was excellent. Everyone enjoyed their food choices. The special John Dory fish entree was delicious. We plan to return on our next visit to NYC.



Hot Stone Sushi, London, UK

I went there for my friend’s birthday. I had heard so many good things about the place, so my expectations were quite high. They, however, managed to go beyond my expectations and we had a fantastic time there. Their dishes were very tasty and all stuff members were super friendly and helpful. I definitely recommend this place! You can’t miss REAL wasabi and Hot stone cooking!



Ciao Checca, Rome, Italy

Wandered upon this restaurant after hours of walking and sightseeing in Rome. Menu was in English and Italian AND featured many gluten free alternatives! I had gluten free pasta with excellent pesto sauce. My friend had a cold pasta salad with some grated vegetables which she said was "all right." Primarily a pasta place (hot and cold) but also salads, soups and some other dishes. Informal but trendy atmosphere and very reasonable price.
